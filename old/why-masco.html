MASCO is a leading conglomerate in Saudi Arabia; with construction and earth moving projects as its core business. It is the leading institution for unprecedented projects that change lives forever. We have one big message when it comes to expertise and the team we hire, to make the world around the people we serve a better place. Everything we do is about changing lives, for communities and our customers alike. From designers who create life changing projects, to construction workers who realize the importance of their performance on millions of humans and the ability of our customers to serve people in the best way ever.

The scope of work for MASCO consists of:Infrastructure Development of Jeddah Housing Complex in Obhur – North of Jeddah including;Earthworks, Civil works for Site Development including Road works, Sidewalks;Asphalt Pavements; Utilities including Water, Drainage & Sewage Distribution Network; Mechanical Works & services; Electrical works including Light Poles.
Irrigation works.

The Total area of the project is nearly 2.5 million m².
The project has been designed according to the latest standards and specifications, as it is located on the way to Madinah in front of the north terminal of King Abdul-Aziz International Airport

building with contemporary methodologies

Digital construction is the use and application of digital tools to improve the process of delivering and operating the built environment” In general terms that will translate into making the delivery, operation and renewal of our built environment safer, more efficient and more collaborative.

Over at MASCO we use state of the art digital technology with the latest tools to achieve the best goals. As it becomes clearer and clearer that digitalization is the future, we want to stay ahead of the wave, staying ahead of our competition.

Using such technologies helps us find solutions more efficiently and adequately, which makes our process faster and more fail proof.

We aim to further improve our digital construction methodologies and tools to keep on developing with the ever-evolving digital age of the 21st century.

our recent innovations in construction

Saudi ARAMCO awarded the contract to Mohammed A. Al Swailem Co. (MASCO) for the Design, Engineering, Installation of Pipeline Leak Detection System (LDS) and Corrosion Monitoring System (CMS), at the Southern and Northern areas of the Eastern Province to enhance safety and performance of pipeline operations.

There are 12 different Main areas namely Shedqum, Ain Dar, Abqaiq, Hawyah, Uthmaniyah, Udaailiyah, Harad, Dhahran, Qatif, Ras Tanoura, Berri, Tanajeeb in the Eastern province and 69 sub areas to install shelters to accommodate the field site processors and modems and many electrical equipment belong to the  two control systems,

The project shall provide for a high performance leak detection system which shall

be installed in the existing “above ground” sour gas pipelines & shall  provide for a  real-time  corrosion  monitoring  system,  which  shall be installed for the existing “above ground” sour liquid service pipelines.

The construction also consists of around 100 km of trenches to install the fiber , power, and instrumentation cables.

There are very serious mechanical works to be accomplished in this Project such as the hot tapping. As per contract, 91 hot tap points need to be done on live pipelines without shutdown.

Worley Parsons is a leading provider of professional services to the resources & energy sectors and complex process industries. They are one of the largest international Design, Engineering and Consulting company approved by ARAMCO. Worley Parsons as our partners, will provide Engineering Detailed Design based on the requirements provided by Aramco
