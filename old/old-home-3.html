Founded

MASCO Group was  founded in 1970 and came into existence through the hard work of  the true visionary, M. Alswailam.

Our founder has started his long journey as a Truck Driver based in Qassem Village in KSA.

Through endless determination, persistency and commitment to deliver a better world, Mr. Alswailam was able to secure the trust of both the Saudi Governments as well as multiple Equipment Suppliers, which gave him the chance to unlock his full potential and create the MASCO Empire.

With its team of calibers, MASCO has withstood many global crisis and has always come out stronger, meeting every single one of its commitments throughout the past 50 years.
‍

Headquarters

As the company grew strong and proud along the years, we have managed to relocate our Head Office from the town of Qassim to the grand city of Riyadh, Saudi Arabia.  

Alongside our Head Office, MASCO Group has managed to secure its presence in 21 other locations spread across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia, serving more than 10 million clients daily.

A Process that is Unique Like No Other

Don’t expect our process to be like that of your everyday construction firm. We are no regular firm. Our process is mainly aimed towards growing communities and tackling big challenges.

Studying the Social Impact

We don’t take on any projects that have no social impact. We don’t do things for money and fame, but we aim to make a change. We start any project by studying the social impact that it would have on the society, then we strategize how we can make this possible.

Studying the Project Scope and Objectives

We then go through our client’s objectives from the project. We want to know what they want to help solve for the society. Then we take those objectives and create a plan which scopes down the projects to a group of milestones that we need to achieve.

Designing and Engineering a Unique Solution

Our strategists, designers, engineers, marketeers, among many others work tirelessly together pulling off what could never be done and make a clear plan, a great design, a previously impossible structure and turn imagination and ideas into tangible, applicable plans and designs.

Laying Down the Basis

We then take the plans and designs of our team and give it to a talented team of engineers and construction workers to turn the 2D plan into the basis of the structure. Laying down a strong basis and infrastructure to make sure that whatever we build later is based on a strong base.

Building the Solution

We turn the infrastructure, the basic structure and start building the solution. All of the hard work becomes reality at this point. Structures are built, grids are laid, and everything turns into a beautiful reality. One that we strive to keep on improving.

Studying the Social Impact

All of the hard work of months and years of work come to this point. Study how our solution aided to solve people’s everyday problems. From bridges and roads like the roads in King Abdullah Sports City, to mega railways like the super-fast Haramain Railway, we only aim to make a difference.

We Have the Proof

We have what proves that what we say and the process we follow works. Numbers never lie, and we’re running a huge number of projects with even huger investments in human wellbeing.

SERVICE RANGE

SPANNING A WIDE RANGE OF SERVICES

As MASCO has deep roots in history, as well as being based in construction, the company had the potential to branch into a wide range of services. The company now has 7 different branches which offer 10+ unique service types to its clients. This also helps make MASCO’s work process as seamless as possible by internally managing a lot of the processes which would’ve been handled by other firms in many other cases.

CONTRACTING AND INDUSTRIAL

MASCO I&C division was primarily to carry out in-house business subcontracts, in both building and waste and water treatments plants. Gradually, it became an independent profit center of the group encountered by opportunities outside the MASCO's business. In 2006, A separate company was formed by the name (Control System Technology Company)- Systech, to be the Electro-mechanical arm of the group.

Systech areas of specially involved Electrical & Mechanical of Building, water technology, Electrical & Instrumentation, Electrical Panel Board manufacturing, Industrial Control Solutions, Building Management System and SCADA. Systech is one of the Pioneer company specialized in electrical and industrial control manufacturing solution provides for oil and gas Industries.

BUILDING

Building hotels, hospitals, colleges is MASCO's specialty with all what involved in architectural, structural finishing and interiors designs works as well as Electromechanical and BMS that is all done with most advanced management tools.

In addition, MASCO has the high caliber management team and a  skilled workforce  capable of Residential, Commercial and Institutional Buildings.

INFRASTRUCTURE AND WATER

The Infrastructures & Water Projects Department in MASCO combine most of expertise and resources needed to give an upscale with sophisticated professional industrial and residential infrastructure projects. Different systems are needed to build a solid infrastructure projects such as water, sewage transmitting networks, storage tanks, water & waste water treatment plants as well as pumping station, streets lighting, electrical substations, power & signals control system, earthmoving as well as rock cutting or explosion, asphalt roads and streets with curbstones, tiles, signboards, street signals, and irrigation and planting.

OPERATIONS, MAINTENANCE AND OUTSOURCING

MASCO has a long and strong experience in road and highway maintenance, MASCO meanwhile is also having water, wastewater and pumping station O&M projects.

MASCO's Vision for O&M activities is to expand it and tackle fields of oil and gas industrial projects. MASCO has recently diversified in the Outsourcing Services offering a group of services in the field of Manpower supply and operations and maintenance service for Public buildings and facilities, Military buildings and facilities, University buildings and facilities, Medical and non-medical maintenance, Airports and Seaports, Banks & Financial Institutions .

We also provide high caliber staff at all levels viz; Admin staff, Technical & specialized staff and Managerial level staff.

CONCRETE BATCHING PLANTS

MASCO by its nature of business has a strong relation with aggregate, sand, cement which form the most essential sources of producing concrete, had been integrated to establish series of concrete batching plants in different locations across the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia. MASCO recently has expanded its production of concrete from supplying its own business to producing commercial quantities for the construction Market.

MASCO believes that it is the right time to go to market taking into consideration the huge demand of concrete across the Kingdom both for private and public projects.

Meanwhile, MASCO has its new facility operating in Jizan to supply mega projects that are lunched around the region. Other successfully built plants has been operating  in Riyadh, Jizan, Dawadmi and Jeddah.

TRANSPORTATION AND SHIPMENT

MASCO's Transportation Division Owns and operate a large fleet of trailers. All trailers are brand new model 2006 & 2007 series. Transportation Division supports MASCO's contracting projects by different applications. These supply  required raw materials such as embankment material, cement, crushed aggregate and Bitumen. In addition, Transportation Division operate as an independent business unit which generate revenue by doing business with other customers.

HIGHWAYS ROADS AND BRIDGES

This division of MASCO was the first to start, going back to 1984 as early as the company started.

MASCO continues to invest heavily in this sector, through massive expansion in terms of  its human resources, their equipments and fleet of more than 1000 different pieces of equipment and plants to serve the roads and bridges construction industry.

MASCO has also successfully extended their roots in railroad construction industry, since 2009.
