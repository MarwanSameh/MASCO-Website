IDEALS OF THE FUTURE

Profit with Purposeful Value

Our strategic direction that will take MASCO to the next level is sustainable profit with purpose. The aim is to increase shareholder value while working toward a more sustainable future for employees, customers and communities. This is how MASCO creates true profit with purposeful value.

Build a Fully Serene Environment

At MASCO we aim to create a serene environment for our customers and the people using the services and constructions we created. Serenity is at the core of our ideology, creating a better life for the society and world at large.

We promise to try our best and do our part in making this world a more sustainable, safer while keeping it human centered.

THE CORE OF HUMAN VALUES

At MASCO our values aren’t based on making more money out of people’s needs, nor is it based on creating fake sense of pleasure for people. We are focused on making people deeply satisfied with what we do, and the way we do it. That’s why we heavily focus on human values, values that we stress on and make sure they are kept throughout all of our departments and heavily adopted in any project we create.

INNOVATION AND RESEARCH

INNOVATION FOR HUMAN WELLBEING

Innovation for Human Wellbeing
The function leads the research and innovation activities in MASCO, identifying new technical solutions in the delivery of projects and making sure that MASCO remains competitive. Through innovation we identify and develop new technical products, services and processes, creating increased value for MASCO and its clientele.
It provides technical input to Skanska's risk management process to reduce risk in relation to complex large-span structures, potential systemic faults and the use of new unproven materials. It also provides technical support for problem solving, and claims resolution, working through a coordinated internal and external network of experts.
Research and innovation are at the core of MASCO’s activities. But unlike other firms, we aim to focus on research and innovation for human wellbeing and not for our own interest. We try to find unique ways to create a sustainable, green future. Through such research we can pinpoint issues in our process that we can make more sustainable.
At MASCO we try to use new materials and processes in our production to help build a brighter future, a future where we see our planet a better place. A planet where we live in harmony with our environment and not against its interests.
