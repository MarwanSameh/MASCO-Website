MASCO is a leading conglomerate in Saudi Arabia; with construction and earth moving projects as its core business. It is the leading institution for unprecedented projects that change lives forever. We are serving more than 10 million customers each and every day through extensively keeping up to what we say. We have one big message when it comes to expertise and the team we hire, to make the world around the people we serve a better place. Everything we do is about changing lives, for communities and our customers alike. From designers who create life changing projects, to construction workers who realize the importance of their performance on millions of humans and the ability of our customers to serve people in the best way ever.
